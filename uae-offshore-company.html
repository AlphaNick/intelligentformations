UAE Offshore Company

  • International reputation as a well regarded offshore tax haven
  • Closed and private registry - it is the law not to disclose information about International Business Companies.
  • Nominee Directors and Shareholders allowed
  • 0% Corporate Tax rate
  • 0% Capital Gain rate
  • 0% Dividend tax to non resident shareholders
  • 0% Inheritence tax
  • 0% wealth tax
  • Political and economic stability
  • Fast incorporation times
  • Shareholder meetings can be held anywhere
  • No Company Audits or financial accounts to be submitted
  • Single-member ownership of a UAE Offshore international company is available
